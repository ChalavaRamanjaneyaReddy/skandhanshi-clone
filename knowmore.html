ABOUT US

"Every successful project is a by-product of our hard work and determination gained by the trust and satisfaction of our customers". Skanda has evolved into Skandhanshi with more potential to acquire successful projects within cities as well for a rapid growth of the organization. Since 2008 SKANDHANSHI has been providing various services and helping people to fulfil their dream of construction.
Head Quartered in Kurnool, Skandhanshi is one of the best construction companies with 12+ years of experience acquiring more than 75,000 customers. As of now, Skandhanshi has constructed an area of 50 lakh sq.ft which includes plots, apartments, gated communities, villas, commercial complexes and many more enticing projects.

Skandhanshi has a legacy of building outstanding real estate projects in Kurnool, Anantapur, Nandyal, Tadipatri, and Kadapa in Andhra Pradesh, along with Ballari and Bengaluru in Karnataka, and Hyderabad in Telangana. It has new upcoming projects in Warangal and Nellore. Skandhanshi has also unfenced the barriers to commence a corporate office in Hyderabad soon for the reinforcement of the company's goals.

Vision

Skandhanshi strives to be an effective and innovative real estate company with a wistful commitment to provide superbly designed and built properties in excellent locations. We focus on building houses, Villas, apartments, and shops obey our trademark high standards and catering to all budgets. After successfully delivering excellent homes to thousands of happy families, we aim to continue the standards and creating magnificent communities.

The mission of Skandhanshi Infra Projects India Pvt.Ltd is to provide an excellent level of service and expertise to the customers in the real estate market. It is dedicated to the highest standards, structure and performance which are essential to fulfil your property dreams.

Foundation

Established in 2018, the Skandhanshi Charitable Trust works towards establishing, managing, and maintaining orphanage homes for the less fortunate children of our country. The foundation provides physical, intellectual, academic, mental, and spiritual development to give orphans a fair chance at life. The foundation also provides good education, books, uniforms, hostel facilities, and other necessities to underprivileged children from all communities.
